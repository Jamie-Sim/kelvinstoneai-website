Hi, I'm Jamie — founder of Kelvinstone AI.

Coming from a background in tech and creative industries, I spent years working with complex digital tools and systems. That work taught me how to think analytically, solve problems methodically, and translate complicated technology into something that actually works in practice.

When AI automation started changing what was possible for small businesses, I noticed something: the trades and property industries — the builders, letting agents, plumbers, and property managers keeping Glasgow and the rest of the UK running — were being left behind. Not because they weren't capable, but because nobody was speaking their language or building tools that fit how they actually work.

That's why I started Kelvinstone AI.

I work with trades and property businesses who are tired of chasing leads that go cold, drowning in admin, or simply wondering how AI could help them without knowing where to start. I build practical automations that handle the repetitive stuff — follow-ups, enquiries, booking — so you can focus on the work you're actually good at.

I'm not here to sell you buzzwords or overcomplicate things. I'm here to help your business keep up with a world that's moving fast, without you having to become a tech expert to do it.
